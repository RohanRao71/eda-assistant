Dataset: Student Performance Dataset

Description:
This dataset contains student academic performance metrics collected from multiple schools.
The purpose is to analyze the relationship between demographic factors and exam outcomes.

Columns:
- gender: Biological sex of the student (male/female)
- race/ethnicity: Self-reported ethnic group
- parental level of education: Highest education level of the parents
- lunch: Type of lunch received (standard or free/reduced)
- test preparation course: Whether the student completed preparation
- math score: Math exam score (0–100)
- reading score: Reading exam score (0–100)
- writing score: Writing exam score (0–100)

Notes:
Scores are standardized.
Missing values were removed prior to ingestion.
